Pricing Details for All Categories

At our superstore in Palava City, Dombivali, India, we strive to offer competitive and transparent pricing for all our products across various categories. We believe in providing our valued customers with high-quality products at affordable prices. Here are the pricing details for the different categories available in our superstore:

 

1. Fresh Produce:

   - Our fresh produce section offers a wide range of fruits and vegetables sourced directly from trusted farmers.

   - Prices may vary depending on the seasonality and availability of the produce.

   - We ensure that our pricing is competitive, reflecting the freshness and quality of the products.

 

2. Dairy and Deli:

   - Our dairy and deli section includes a variety of milk, cheese, yogurt, butter, and deli meats.

   - Prices may vary based on the brand, type, and size of the products.

 

3. Pantry Staples:

   - Our pantry staples section consists of essential items such as rice, grains, pasta, pulses, oils, and spices.

   - Prices may vary depending on the brand, quantity, and quality of the products.

 

4. Packaged Foods:

   - Our packaged foods section offers a diverse range of snacks, canned goods, sauces, condiments, and ready-to-eat meals.

   - Prices may vary based on the brand, size, and type of the products.

 

5. Beverages:

   - Our beverage section includes a variety of soft drinks, juices, tea, coffee, and energy drinks.

   - Prices may vary depending on the brand, size, and packaging of the beverages.

 

6. Household Essentials:

   - Our household essentials section comprises cleaning supplies, personal care items, toiletries, and paper products.

   - Prices may vary based on the brand, size, and quantity of the products.

 

7. Wine and Beer:

   - Our wine and beer section offers a diverse selection of wines and beers to suit different preferences and occasions.

   - Wine and beer prices may vary depending on factors such as brand, type, region, vintage, and packaging.

 

Please note that prices are subject to change based on market conditions, supplier agreements, and any applicable taxes or levies. We continuously strive to offer competitive pricing and may occasionally provide promotions or discounts on select products.

 

We are committed to providing our customers with transparent pricing and excellent value for their money. Should you have any specific inquiries about pricing or require assistance in choosing the right products, our knowledgeable staff members are always available to assist you.
